About Us

Etuition.lk is an online education platform designed to cover the educational needs in Sri Lanka. The platform targets improving student's convenience in finding a suitable tutor and the tutor's convenience in promoting their classes. The recent changes in the global environment have forced the Sri Lanka education system to find alternative ways to carry on. Teachers are forced to change the teaching methods and students have to rely on technological devices to learn. In order to meet this technological demand for education locally and internationally, we have established a common web technology platform to minimize discrimination in education. Simply, our advanced online learning platform is targeted to meet the educational needs of both students and teachers to find the right class and students. Students can look into suitable online or local tuition classes for any subject irrespective of the language medium and syllabus. At the same time, teachers can promote their classes with us to gain a large audience. Furthermore, we offer genuine online teacher support, free online courses for students and online tuition programmes for teachers.
